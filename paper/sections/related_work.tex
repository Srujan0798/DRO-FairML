\citet{solans2021poisoning} introduced the first poisoning attacks on algorithmic fairness using heuristic strategies. Our work extends this with exact gradient computation and PGD optimization. \citet{hashimoto2018fairness} formulated fairness within DRO; our evaluation reveals that DRO's theoretical guarantees may not translate to all data modalities under adversarial corruption. \citet{madry2018towards} established PGD as the standard for adversarial robustness; we adapt this framework to fairness metrics.
